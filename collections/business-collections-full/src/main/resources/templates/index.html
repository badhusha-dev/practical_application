About This Demo

This application demonstrates all major Java Collection objects in real business scenarios:

  • List: ArrayList (fast random access) and LinkedList (efficient insertions/deletions)
  • Set: HashSet (fast lookups), TreeSet (sorted), LinkedHashSet (insertion order)
  • Map: HashMap (fast lookups) and TreeMap (sorted by key)
  • Queue: LinkedList (FIFO) and PriorityQueue (priority-based)
  • Stack: LIFO operations for completed tasks
  • Deque: Double-ended queue operations
  • Vector: Thread-safe operations with Enumeration
